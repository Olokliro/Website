Olokliro is an ambitious project to simplify integrations, speed up development and enable businesses to quickly consume webservices. Olokliro will offer cloud based project management, and visual editors for managing and mapping integrations on top of our powerful MidPoint Engine. Use your existing API or have Olokliro generate library for you.

Public trials are expected to begin late in the 2nd or 3rd quarter of 2017. If you are interested in trialing Olokliro please reach out to us through our contact page.

This site will be updated as more information becomes available and milestones are reached.
