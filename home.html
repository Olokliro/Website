Olokliro is an ambitious project to simplify integrations, speed up development and enable businesses to quickly consume webservices. Olokliro will offer cloud based project management, and visual editors for managing and mapping integrations on top of our powerful MidPoint Engine.

Public trials are expected to begin in mid 2017. If you are interested in trialing Olokliro please reach out to us through our contact page.

This site will be updated as more information becomes available and milestones are reached.

Features Preview
Organization Managment

Create and manage your organization. Claims based authorization allows for granular control of access rights to projects and solutions in your organization. Quickly and easily create users, and assign them to projects.

Project Management

Choose from a number of different flavours of projects to create. Intergrations class library, integrations as a service, data migration and data synchronization to begin with. All projects are based of our drag and drop Visual Mapping and Editor tool, but offer different strengths based on the needs of your organization.

Visual Mapping and Editor Tool

The front end of our service, and principal tool used to create all projects. An easy to use, drag and drop interface for mapping integrations. Creating mapping templates from scratch or load predefined templates, share and explore templates throughout your organization or even with the entire community.

User Forum

We, at Olokliro, believe customer support and a rich and robust community is the best way for a development product to succeed. Naturally a forum will be created for users to meet and discuss their problems and share their solutions.
